Intro

A Little Story of my Life

My story of becoming a software engineer started when I met David Le. He and I had a great friendship from the beginning with countless late night gaming sessions, followed by grief and regret the following morning at work. We were both always hungry for something more than our current occupation in hospitality, he and I were always on the lookout for entrepeneurship and growth. I was introduced to App Academy by David. I can say for certain that this moment was my 'Big Bang' moment that changed the trajectory of my professional life.

Work

Pynterest

This full-stack application is a clone of Pinterest with some liberties taken in styling and funtionality to fit a brand new application as opposed to an established one. This was my final project and capstone in App Academy's program.

Core functionalities

Users can:
- Create, read, update, or delete 'Pyns' which are a parallel to photos in a normal picture application.
- Create, read, update, or delete 'Boards' which act like albums for photos to live in.
- Create, read, update, or delete comments on each 'Pyn.'

Coming soon

- Instant messaging between users
- Carousel slideshows
- Profile pages for all users

FindTable

Another full-stack application that mimicked OpenTable. This was a group project that three of my other cohort-mates and I created with countless hours of debugging and learning the best ways to work in a group to achieve a completed application.

Core functionalities

Users can:
- Create, read, update, or delete restaurant listings
- Create, read, update, or delete restaurant reviews
- Create, read, update, or delete restaurant reservations
- Create or delete favorites on restaurants

Trickr

This full-stack application was built with technologies that are different from the former two. This was my first ever full-stack application and I'm proud of the progression of my work.

Core functionalities

Users can:
- Create, read, update, or delete illusions. Illusions are a parallel to photos on Flickr.
- Create, read, update, or delete comments on illusions

About

I'm a software engineer with a fairly clean slate. Being an engineer who is malleable in their learning and always looking to mentally stimulate themself is who I aim to be. I've always taken pride in knowing my work ethic and hunger for knowledge has been par-none. One of my most motivating forces in life is making sure my dogs get all the treats and toys they could ever dream of, but also making sure that my work has a positive impact on the world. I knew that with technology becoming ever-more prevalent in today's society, software engineering was the best way to achieve that goal. I guess it's kind of a bonus that I just absolutely love this field and everything it can offer!
